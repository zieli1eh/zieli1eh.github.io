Digtal Illustration

This class was one where we were given a brief into to multiple types of design. The 4th assignment was to create an illustration on Adobe Illustraor and it was one that I enjoyed a lot. Unfortunately, I was not able to use my entire concept, as the instructor believed that I would likely not have the time to complete it all. SO the final version is scaled down from what the draft was.

The one limit on this assignment was to keep the color palette to a minimal of what I believe was only supposed to be around 3 colors, including different shades of the color. I used the pen tool for most all the work on here, other than some shapes that I curved or straightened. I will say it was challenging to get used to the pen tool instead of just drawing with a paint brush, but I found it made it easier to create smoother defined lines.
